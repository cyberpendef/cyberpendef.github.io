Personal Introduction
Hi, I'm Hidayatullah — and I genuinely enjoy solving tech problems.

I've been the person friends and family call when something goes wrong with their computer since I was a teenager. What started as a natural curiosity for how systems work grew into a professional career in IT and cybersecurity — and eventually into CyberPenDef, my own practice for delivering affordable, expert-level digital services to people who deserve better than confusing, overpriced tech support.

I hold a CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker) certification from EC-Council and bring 3 years of professional IT and cybersecurity experience alongside 8+ years of hands-on personal computer and IT troubleshooting experience. I've worked on real-world system administration, participated in cybersecurity labs and Capture the Flag (CTF) challenges, and built automation tools using Python, C, and other programming languages. I stay sharp by continuously learning and practicing in environments that mirror real attacker techniques.

I started CyberPenDef because I believe cybersecurity and reliable IT support shouldn't be the exclusive privilege of large enterprises with big budgets. Individuals, freelancers, and small business owners deserve the same protection and quality of service — delivered in plain language, at a fair price, with complete transparency.
